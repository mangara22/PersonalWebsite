Volunteer Youth Commissioner

Served for two terms as a Youth Commissioner for the Burnsville PNRC

From July 2019 - June 2021, I volunteered for the Burnsville Parks and Natural Resources Commission. I gave helpful insight on what can be done to improve the community's parks, lakes, trails, and other natural spaces from a high schooler's perspective.
